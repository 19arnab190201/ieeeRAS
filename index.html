IEEE-RAS

IEEE - Robotics and Automation (RAS) is a student chapter in Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT), Vellore with its members being multi-disciplinary enthusiasts of novel computational practices and precision robotics. The chapter is a formidable formation of engineering students who have worked on several major projects and have organized several successful technical events in the university.


With constant internal efforts to build something innovative, engaging with other inventors, hobbyists through its lively online community, 'Innerve' on Skillenza and raising fellow junior students to be proficient in several technical fields via several workshops and hands-on sessions, IEEE-RAS has come up to be one of the leading international student chapters here in VIT.
